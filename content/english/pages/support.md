Decentralized finance holds the promise of secure, reliable and globally accessible public financial infrastructure. The DeFi Collective is at the forefront of this movement, directly supporting DeFi protocols and working with the broader financial industry, policymakers and the public. To continue this work, the Ants need your support.

### Donations

The Collective relies on community funding to sustain its activities and grow its impact across the DeFi ecosystem:

- We accept donations in ETH and standard tokens (ERC-20, ERC-721, ERC-1155, etc.) at [grantsfortheants.eth](https://etherscan.io/address/0xDc6f869d2D34E4aee3E89A51f2Af6D54F0F7f690) (0xDc6f869d2D34E4aee3E89A51f2Af6D54F0F7f690) on Ethereum Mainnet, Arbitrum, Base, Optimism, Polygon-POS and Polygon-zkEVM. All donations are subject to our [Donation Terms](../donation-terms).
- We also welcome other forms of funding such as grants, research funds or institutional support from public and private organizations, DeFi projects and the broader crypto community. To discuss a specific form of support, reach out to us directly.

### Other ways to contribute

Have another way to support our mission? [Reach out to us on Discord](https://discord.gg/Z467Ehv6VU) - we'd love to hear from you.

### Disclaimer

*The DeFi Collective is a non-profit association. Donations may not be deductible for tax purposes under applicable law. No goods or services are provided in return for any donation. Please refer to our [Donation Terms](../donation-terms) for details.*
